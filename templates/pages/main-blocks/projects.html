Empowerment Online

Empowerment Online is an extension of Empowerment Counseling Services, a community based agency dedicated to supporting clients mental health needs for over 20 years. Our aim is to assist clients in successfully overcoming challenges and hardships to create effective, affordable and accessible solutions to start living their very best lives, ever.
